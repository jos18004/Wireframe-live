Ahara Hakini Joseph – Personal Site Plan

Site Plan Overview

This document outlines the structure, design, and content of my personal website, including the intended audience, navigation, style guide, and wireframes.

Purpose

This website will showcase my skills, education, personal projects, and resume. It is designed to act as a digital portfolio to highlight who I am and what I can offer professionally.

Audience

The primary audience includes recruiters, instructors, internship coordinators, and fellow students who want to explore my academic and project background.

Navigation

  • Home: Welcome message, site overview
  • About Me: Biography, background, interests
  • Projects: Descriptions and links to personal projects
  • Contact: Email, LinkedIn, contact form

Site Content Overview

  • Home Page: Short welcome message, photo, site purpose
  • About Me Page: Academic background, interests, profile photo
  • Projects Page: Cards or sections showing individual projects with title, short description, and link
  • Contact Page: Email, LinkedIn profile link, optional contact form

Color Scheme

  • Navy Blue: #004080 (Primary)
  • Gold: #FFD700 (Accent)
  • White: #FFFFFF (Background)

Fonts

  • Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif
